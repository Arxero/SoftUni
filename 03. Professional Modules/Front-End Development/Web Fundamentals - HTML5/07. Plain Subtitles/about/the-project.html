PLAIN SUBTITLES

Overview

Plain Subtitles is a website made with the sole purpose to make life easier for people who want to watch free movies online with subtitles. In movie websites like FMOVIES.to, 5movies.to, YesMovies.to and many others. At the current time you are able to import subtitles in any video hosted on openload.co and streamango.com, directly from their video players.

Just copy the subtitles link from our conveniently located table in every movie page.

Providers

All subs at the current time are hosted on gist.github.com and pastebin.com, using their raw form of text file hosting. Images for the movies are also outsourced from their IMDB page.

The Future

In the current stage of the development is nothing more than proof of concept, as it has some bugs and the future is unclear.
